Our Annual Art Exhibition

We are excited to host our annual Art Exhibition in the heart of winter.

Over the course of four evenings visitors can immerse themselves in the creative art work of our students over the past year. This event gives a great opportunity to our students and teachers to show and sell their work to the public.

Enjoy sherry, wine and hearty home-made food. Bonfires and music create the perfect atmosphere for an evening out.

Be sure to visit us to take your evening from the ordinary to the extraordinary.
