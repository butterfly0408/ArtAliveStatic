Our Annual Art Exhibition

We are excited to host our annual Art Exhibition in the heart of winter.

Over the course of four evenings visitors can immerse themselves in the creative art work of our students over the past year. This event gives a great opportunity to our students and teachers to show and sell their work to the public.

Enjoy sherry, wine and hearty home-made food. Bonfires and music create the perfect atmosphere for an evening out.

Be sure to visit us to take your evening from the ordinary to the extraordinary.

Oil Painting Workshops

Join us on an artistic journey as we dive into a series of engaging oil painting workshops. Each session promises an opportunity for you to unleash your creativity and create your own masterpiece. Mark your calendars for these inspiring dates and themes:

2 March: Back to Basics for Beginners
Rediscover the fundamental techniques that form the foundation of captivating artwork.

6 April: Exploring the Importance of Colour
Immerse yourself in the world of color theory and discover how it can elevate your artistic expression.

1 May: Seascapes Unveiled
Capture the beauty of the sea as we guide you through the creation of mesmerizing seascape paintings.

29 June: Tonal Harmony with Warm and Cold Colours
Delve into the nuances of tonal value, exploring the interplay between warm and cold colors for dynamic compositions.

7 September: Perspective Matters
Learn the art of creating depth and dimension in your paintings through the mastery of perspective.

5 October: Palette Knife Mastery
Unleash the unique textures and effects achievable with palette knife techniques, adding a new dimension to your art.
