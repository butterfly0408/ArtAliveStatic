Mercia Kruger

I have been an artist and student for more than ten years.

I am particularly interested in creating contemporary pieces and exploring the photo-realism side of art. With all of my art projects I challenge myself to pay attention to detail. I am mostly inspired by out of the ordinary twists and details to art.

It is a privilege to share my knowledge, tips and tricks with the students at Art Alive. Every person is treated as an individual and is encouraged to develop their own unique style.

I truly believe that anyone can be an artist. If you believe in yourself then you are already halfway there. You are capable of doing so much more than you think you can do.
