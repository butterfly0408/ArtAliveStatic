Rourke Lyte-Mason

I am Rourke Lyte-Mason. I joined Art Alive Art School when I was 16 years old.

In 2013, I took a gap year and attended Art Alive Art School where I realized I wanted to be an artist. I started studying Fine Art at the University of Johannesburg in 2014 and in 2015 I won the Thami Mnyele Painting Merit Award.

In 2017 & 2018, I made the top 100 in both the Thami Mnyele & Sasol New Signatures. I represented the University of Johannesburg at the Association of Arts Pretoria Student Exhibition in 2017 and 2018.

I graduated with my National Diploma and my BA Degree for Fine Art at the University of Johannesburg in 2017 and 2018 and became an art teacher at Benhale Academy Christian Private School and worked there till 2020 and joined Springs Boys' High School in 2021.
