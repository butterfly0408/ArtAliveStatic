Art Alive Art School Blog

Welcome Back

It was with great excitement that we re-opened in January, welcoming back familiar faces, and embracing the fresh energy of new talents. During our recent teacher's meeting, we discussed the exciting roadmap that will guide our artistic learning throughout the year.

In 2024, we're not just teaching art; we're crafting an experience. A journey through diverse art mediums, all while ensuring a steadfast focus on the art elements and principles that form the foundation of every great work of art.

What sets our school apart is the invitation extended to artists throughout the year. No need to wait for a specific enrollment period – the door is always open for those eager to immerse themselves in a world of artistic exploration. Whether you're a seasoned artist or just discovering your passion, our inclusive atmosphere invites you to join at any moment, making art an integral part of your life whenever inspiration strikes.

At the end of January, we will be announcing our Art Workshop Programme. These workshops are open to anyone wanting to learn more about painting.

Picture a cozy ambiance, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, and a canvas awaiting your touch. In each session, we delve into themed exploration, guiding participants step by step as they create their own masterpieces. In these workshops, you'll paint alongside us, exchanging ideas with fellow artists. Whether you're a seasoned painter or a novice, our workshops offer a welcoming space to nurture your creativity, where every stroke is a learning experience.

Join us in this delightful fusion of art and coffee culture, where the journey is as enriching as the destination, and creativity thrives in the heart of Benoni's coffee scene.

Starting February 1, 2024, keep an eye out for our "Teacher of the Month" feature. This is a tribute to the dedicated Artists who inspire and guide our students towards artistic excellence.

Join us on this journey of artistic exploration, where every stroke and every shade contribute to growing your artistic talent.

News on our workshops and Teacher of the month will be added here and on our social media platforms.

Till next time

Elaine 15 January 2024

Happy New Year

As we say goodbye to another incredible year at Art Alive Art School, we reflect on the canvases we've filled, the skills we've learned, and the creativity that has flourished.

I am reading a book by Emily P. Freeman, where she talks about uncovering the Art we were made to live and that we should move towards that which makes us feel alive. For me spending time with loved ones and creating my Art always makes me feel alive.

In her book, A million little ways, Emily quotes this from John Acuff: “More often than not, finding out what you love doing most is about recovering an old love or an inescapable truth that has been silenced for years, even decades. When you come to your dream job, your thing, it is rarely a first encounter. It's usually a reunion. So instead of setting out to discover this thing you love doing, you've got to change your thinking and set out to recover it, maybe even rescue it!”

This quote made me think that all of our students should be proud of the reunion that they have made with their art. May we all continue to explore our creativity.

I wish all our talented artists a New Year filled with boundless inspiration, vibrant palettes, and the joy of endless artistic possibilities.

May your brushes dance across the canvas of 2024!

Happy New Year from myself and our wonderful teachers. We look forward to welcoming you back at the School.
